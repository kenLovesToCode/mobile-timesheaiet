priceTag — Design Directions

Calm / minimal • Grocery Green • Camera-first with fast form fallback

How to evaluate

  • Scan feels calm and never “stuck” (fallback is obvious around ~5s).
  • Results reduce doubt: product name is prominent, barcode + timestamp are visible but secondary.
  • Missing is actionable (quiet state, clear CTA) and saving updates instantly.
  • Choose a direction or mix elements across directions.
